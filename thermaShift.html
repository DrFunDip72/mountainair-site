ThermaShift Technology

Discover the innovative technology behind our products.

What is the ThermaShift Technology?

Imagine being able to adjust the warmth of your tent or jacket at will, much like turning up or down a thermostat. This is no longer a fantasy, thanks to our innovative technology that integrates switchable thermal properties into everyday camping gear. Our solution leverages a multilayered assembly that can be inflated and deflated at low pressures, allowing for dynamic control over heat transfer.

By altering radiative transport—essentially how heat moves through materials—we've created an adaptable insulation system that's perfect for outdoor enthusiasts

How does it work?

The magic behind this technology lies in its simplicity and efficiency. At its core, it uses layers of reflective material that change orientation when air pressure is applied. This action modulates the way heat is either retained or released, depending on your needs.

When you inflate the system, it switches to a mode that reflects infrared radiation, keeping you warm by trapping heat. Conversely, when deflated, it allows more heat to escape, providing cooling relief during warmer conditions.

Watch Our ThermaShift Technology in Action

Unlike traditional insulation methods that are static and often bulky, our solution adapts in real-time to environmental changes without requiring significant energy input. It's easy to install and can be integrated into existing products with minimal effort. Whether you're camping in the desert or hiking through cooler climates, this technology ensures optimal thermal comfort with minimal energy expenditure.
